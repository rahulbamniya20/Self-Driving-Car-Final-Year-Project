## 📌 Introduction

This project presents the development of an artificial agent capable of playing a car racing game using advanced reinforcement learning techniques. We aimed to create an intelligent agent that can efficiently navigate and control a car in various racing environments, adapting to different tracks and optimizing its performance over time.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The challenge was to develop an AI that could successfully navigate and control a car in a simulated racing environment, dealing with diverse tracks and dynamically changing conditions to maintain high performance and learning efficiency.

---

## 🔍 Literature Review

We explored various reinforcement learning strategies, virtual simulators like TORCS, CARLA, and OpenAI Gym, alongside innovative training performances and algorithms to base our approach on cutting-edge research and methodologies.

---

## 💡 Motivation

Driven by the advancements in deep learning and autonomous vehicle technologies, we sought to apply similar concepts to a controlled gaming environment, providing insights and potential applications for real-world autonomous driving solutions.

---

## 📖 Methodology

Utilizing a deep reinforcement learning framework, focusing on designing a reward system that encourages optimal decision-making and continuous improvement through a detailed training process.

## 🚗 Design and Implementation

### System Design

Our system architecture integrates the reinforcement learning model with a simulated environment for iterative training and performance optimization.

### Training Process

Utilizing the PPO algorithm for stability and efficiency, we trained our agent with a CNN policy, focusing on continuous control and decision-making based on environmental feedback.
